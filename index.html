Welcome to my website! I am a third-year student at UCLA majoring in Mathematics / Economics with a Specialization and Computing and an intended minor in accounting. I am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area and am dedicated to pursuing the actuarial career.

Towards the end of high school, I realized I wanted to study mathematics, and the actuarial field was something I took an immediate interest in. Ever since, I've wanted to pursue the multidisciplinary approach to applying mathematics, economics, finance, and computer programming. This summer, I was the actuarial intern at Capital Insurance Group, a property and casualty insurance company operating on the West Coast. During my internship, I was exposed to pricing, reserving, and predictive modelling. I hope to further my actuarial knowledge with another property and casualty internship next summer.

I currently serve as Director of Professional Development of UCLA's Bruin Actuarial Society, the university's premier organization for student actuaries. Please feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect. I can be reached at kevinzhu98@gmail.com.
